Common questions

How do I reset my data?
Go to Settings inside the app and use the reset or delete option. All data is stored on your device.

Is there an Android version?
Not yet. Meridia is currently iOS only.

How do I restore a purchase?
On the paywall or subscription screen, tap "Restore Purchases." Apple handles all billing.
