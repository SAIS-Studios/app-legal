Privacy Policy · Last updated May 28, 2026

Quaestor is built privacy-first. This policy explains, in plain language, what the app does and does not do with your information.

What we collect

Nothing. Quaestor does not collect, transmit, or sell any personal information. There is no account and no login.

Where your data lives

  • Your study progress (scores, streaks, settings) is stored on your device.
  • If you use the same Apple ID on more than one device, progress can sync privately across your own devices through Apple iCloud. That data stays within your private iCloud and is never sent to us.

What we do not do

  • No advertising.
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • No third-party data sharing.
  • No data leaves your device except your own optional iCloud sync.

Children

Quaestor contains no objectionable content and collects no data, so it is safe for all ages.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated date above will change. Questions? Email us any time.
