Privacy Policy · Last updated May 30, 2026

Educolor is built privacy-first. This policy explains, in plain language, what the app does and does not do with your information.

What we collect

Nothing.

Where your data lives

  • Your nothing is stored on your device.
  • If you use the same Apple ID on more than one device, data can sync privately through Apple iCloud. That data stays within your private iCloud and is never sent to us.

What we do not do

  • No advertising.
  • No analytics or tracking SDKs.
  • No third-party data sharing.
  • No data leaves your device except your own optional iCloud sync.

Children

Educolor contains no objectionable content and collects no personal data, so it is safe for all ages.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated date above will change. Questions? Email us any time.
